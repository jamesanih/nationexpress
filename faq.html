FAQ

Find answers to the questions we get asked most often below.

How does NationExpress24 work?
It's easy. Complete the price estimate form on pricing page, place your order, then a rider/driver shows up to pick up your package and deliver it.
How does NationExpress24 calculate prices?
Pricing is based on distance, parcel weight or size and speed i.e. Instant, Same Day or Next Day.
How do I pay?
You can either pay with cash or bank transfer. For cash, this will be collected at the point of pickup. Please ensure cash is ready before the rider arrives at the pickup location.
Can I trust NationExpress24?
All packages delivered by NationExpress24 are fully insured against theft and damages. In the case of damages or exceptions, we perform thorough investigations and ensure you are adequately compensated.
Will I get proof of delivery?
Yes. All our drivers and riders request a delivery code from receiver, before handing over the package. This delivery code is automatically generated and sent to the receiver when a delivery request is placed on our system. The receiver can give this code to someone else to receive the package if they are not available. When this is done, a message will be sent to you to confirm delivery.
How do I know the cost of my delivery?
We let you know how much to pay before you checkout.
What's the delivery time?
Instant Delivery has a 3 hour delivery guarantee from pickup time. Same Day has a 5 hour delivery time from pickup time.
Can I track my parcel online?
Our online parcel tracking system let's you know the current location of your item.
What locations do you serve?
We currrently operate within Lagos but use our partners to deliver outside Lagos.
